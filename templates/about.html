Welcome to StepPy
StepPy is an automatic limit calculator designed to simplify and streamline complex mathematical calculations. Developed as part of our Modeling of Physical Phenomena course, StepPy is an innovative tool that automates the calculation of limits, providing precise and detailed results. Our website is intuitive and free, ideal for students and professionals seeking efficiency and clarity in their studies and work. Our mission is to facilitate the understanding and resolution of mathematical problems, contributing to the educational and professional advancement of our users.
About us
We are a trio of third-semester Computer Science students at the Pontifical Catholic University of Paraná, united by our passion for programming and problem-solving. Our team consists of Isabella Vanderlinde Berkembrock, Lucca Fabricio Magalhães, and Vitor Nicoletti. We share a common enthusiasm for developing innovative solutions and tackling complex challenges. Our collaboration is based on a strong interest in creating tools that make users' lives easier, combining our theoretical knowledge with practical programming skills. Through StepPy, we demonstrate our commitment to contributing to the field of education and technology, always striving to improve and expand our technical capabilities. We present StepPy with great enthusiasm, believing in its potential to transform how mathematical calculations are performed. We are dedicated to continuously learning and evolving as developers, always ready to face new challenges and create impactful solutions.
